# Privacy Policy – TimeFast

**Last updated:** 29 January 2026

---

## 1. Introduction

TimeFast (“the App”) is a wellness and lifestyle application designed to help users manage and follow personalized intermittent fasting schedules and healthy habit routines.

TimeFast is intended to support informed decision-making and personal organization related to fasting and wellness habits.

TimeFast is **not a medical application** and does not provide medical advice, diagnoses, or treatments.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

TimeFast is designed with **privacy by default** principles.

### 2.1 Personal Data

The App does **not** collect, store, or transmit personally identifiable information, including but not limited to:

- Name  
- Email address  
- Phone number  
- Location data  
- Account credentials  

### 2.2 User-Provided Data

Any information entered by the user (such as fasting schedules, preferences, or habit-related inputs) is:

- Stored locally on the user’s device  
- Not transmitted to external servers  
- Not accessible by the developer or third parties  

---

## 3. Use of Artificial Intelligence (AI)

TimeFast may use **artificial intelligence–based features** to enhance the user experience.

### Purpose of AI Usage

AI is used exclusively to:

- Generate general recommendations  
- Personalize app behavior based on user preferences  
- Improve usability and clarity of informational content  

### Important Clarifications

- AI features do **not** provide medical, nutritional, or clinical advice  
- AI outputs are **informational only**  
- AI does **not** replace professional medical guidance  
- No decisions with legal or medical consequences are made by AI  

### Data and AI

- AI features operate without accessing personally identifiable information  
- User data is **not** used to train AI models  
- No sensitive health data is shared with AI systems  

---

## 4. Data Sharing

TimeFast does **not** share user data with third parties.

Specifically:

- No advertising networks are used  
- No analytics or tracking services are integrated  
- No third-party SDKs collect user information  

---

## 5. Data Storage and Security

- All user data is stored locally on the device  
- The App relies on Apple’s iOS security mechanisms and encryption  
- No external databases or cloud storage are used for personal data  

While no system can guarantee absolute security, TimeFast follows industry best practices to minimize risk.

---

## 6. Health Disclaimer

TimeFast is a **wellness and lifestyle support tool**.

The App:

- Does not provide medical diagnoses  
- Does not recommend treatments  
- Does not substitute professional medical advice  

Users should consult qualified healthcare professionals before making health-related decisions, especially those involving fasting or dietary changes.

---

## 7. Children’s Privacy

TimeFast is not intended for children under the age of 13.

The App does not knowingly collect data from children.

---

## 8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect:

- App feature changes  
- Legal or regulatory requirements  

When updates occur, the **“Last updated”** date will be revised. Continued use of the App implies acceptance of the updated policy.
